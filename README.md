AURA – AI-Powered Life Operating System

Your Life. Perfectly Orchestrated.

AURA OS is an AI-powered Life Operating System that unifies productivity, learning, finance, health, communication, and family management into one intelligent platform. Instead of switching between multiple applications, users can manage their entire digital life from a single dashboard powered by AI, automation, and real-time synchronization.

Features

* AI Tutor with multi-model AI support
* Gmail Integration (Read, Compose, Reply, AI Summaries)
* Smart Calendar & Scheduling
* AI-Powered Task Management
* Learning Center with Courses & Assignments
* Smart Notes with AI Integration
* Personal Finance Manager
* Health & Wellness Tracker
* Family Hub with Live Collaboration
* AI Workflow Automation
* Real-Time Analytics Dashboard
* Global Search Across All Modules
* Interactive Data Visualization (Recharts)
* Dark & Light Mode
* Fully Responsive Design
